Attic

The attic is a prohibited and upstairs room that is just by the locker rooms in the back hall of the main and aux. gym. It is label as storage, though nothing is stored in there. It has big ventilation pipes and a switch board that controls something unknown. It is roughly 50 square feet and the ceiling is about 8 above the ground. The big ventilation pipes have a little 6 inch by 8 inch door that allows you look inside, this pipe is the main pipe that provides air conditioning to the main gym.

 To get to this attic, go toward the locker rooms. To the left of the laundry room and boys locker rooms, there is a door labeled ' Storage '. Pull the door open and immediately to the right is a yellow and steep stair case that leads up to this attic. This area is, however, prohibited and sometimes locked.
